Tony Bermea

I am a Front-end Developer and Digtal / UX Designer based in Vancouver, BC.

I've always been curious about understanding how things work. As a digital designer, I wanted to push myself and understand the technical elements of my day to day so I got into development, first on my own, then I earned a diploma in Web and Mobile Development.

When not coding, I am watching movies, making some music or skateboarding with my frineds.

I consider myself a curious and creative person, so in my spare time I like to work on side projects. I'm constantly learning something new to improve my skillset.

A few thoughts on (web) design:
I believe design is not just how things look and feel but also how they work. And to build this culture of design and design thinking throughout a development team is sometimes hard but crucial in creating not only the thing right, but also creating the right thing.

Let's work togheter!
