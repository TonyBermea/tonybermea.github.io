Many people believe that when buying an NFT they are buying an image or a video, and this is not the case.

People need to understand that when they buy NFT art right now it is nothing more than a series of instructions on how to access or download an image. There is a knowledge gap between buyer and seller right now that is being used to exploit people.


This project is “educational art”, because it is playing with the fact that someone can “steal your NFT" by right clicking.
